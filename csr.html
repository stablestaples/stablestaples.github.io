CSR Email template

If you are on this page, it's probably because you've seen our call for help, and are willing help us approach your company's CSR/Leadership team to see if your company would be able to support us. Thank you for your support. To help make this easier, we have provided a template for you below. Feel free to modify it to suit the message you want to deliver to your CSR team.

Dear CSR team,

Hope all of you are keeping well. The COVID-19 situation has no doubt disrupted our lives, but we are fairly lucky to still have our jobs and have a stable income, and I'm rather grateful to the company for making that happen. However, there are families out there who are not as lucky, who have been relying on part time/overtime income to support their families, and today, they are finding it difficult to make ends meet.

I've previously supported Project Stable Staples(https://projectstablestaples.sg/) who are providing support for these families who are mostly staying in the public rental communities. They struggle from paycheck to paycheck, and the current situation is making things harder for them. I'm reaching out to check if it will be possible for the company to support this non profit organisation in Singapore, as it is something close to my heart.

You'll be able to reach out to them via their website, or their email at projectstablestaples.sg [at] gmail.com.

Thank you

Love in COVID times. #SGUnited
